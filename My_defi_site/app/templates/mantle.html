Mantle

Mantle is an innovative layer-2 solution designed to enhance the performance and scalability of blockchain applications. It offers a range of features that make it an ideal choice for developers building on blockchain.

What is Mantle?

Mantle is designed to address the limitations of existing blockchain platforms by providing higher throughput and lower transaction costs. It achieves this through advanced consensus mechanisms and efficient data structures.

Use Cases for Mantle

  • Decentralized Exchanges (DEXs)
  • Payment Networks
  • Supply Chain Management
